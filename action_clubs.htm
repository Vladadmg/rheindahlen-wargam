Hohne
Nick, Padge, Mark and yes we believe we might even see Tyrone again, plus a few other lads want to do a Hammering Iron ACW naval game. In between times Nick wants to do his large WWII game (we believe it’s just to show off his prowess at painting vehicles instead of figures). It’s called Panzer Marsh and is in 20mm scale. Don’t forget that March 26th is the old man’s birthday (Mark Richardson I mean) do buy him a coke or something.

Lastly Charles Gray and Dougie Douglas will present a 1/48th scale demo game based on Arnhem. In all 7 or 8 members coming.
