Back by popular demand:

RE-ACTION

The wargames show formerly known as Action
 

16th to 18th March 2012

Rheindahlen Rooms, Wellington Road 31, 41179 Mönchengladbach

  

Due to changing circumstances which have delayed the closure of Rheindahlen Military Complex the Rheindahlen Wargames Club feel we are in a good position to run our show ACTION one more time.

 

  • Wargaming in most scales, periods and genres with many clubs providing participation & demonstration games.
     

  • Wargames Tournaments subject to demand & organizers
     

  • Gamers from across mainland Europe
     

  • Trade stands from the UK, Germany, the Netherlands and Belgium
     

  • Snack Bar, Free entry and free parking
     

  • Prize Draw/Tombola

 


The Rheindahlen Wargames Club hopes you to see you there
and that you enjoy the show!
