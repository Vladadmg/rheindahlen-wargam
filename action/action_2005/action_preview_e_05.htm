A games convention held by the military village at the Rheindahlen rooms, JHQ, Mönchengladbach, over the weekend of 12th and 13th March.

Free Entrance and Free Parking

Take part in the Rheindahlen Wargames Club simulation of the
Normandy landing 1944. If you thought Custer had it bad last year just wait till you get into our allied landing simulation! Oh yes and the table size does not get any smaller (approx. 15 quare metres!).

BRING & BUY
Not quite the largest Bring and Buy in the world but damn well near it! Clear out your unwanted lead and convert it to cash. Leave a few bob in your pocket for our large Raffle (tombola) as well. If you want to leave your items with the club prior to the weekend of the convention please contact Nick or Vic first.

TIMES
The club and its members will be on the site from 1000 Friday morning for you to start to set up. Friday is arrival and set up day although the DBM competition guys have their first round on Friday evening. For visitors the club site will be open from saturday 9.00 to 18.30 and on sunday from 9.30 to 15.00 hrs.

BEDS
If you need overnight accommodation then get in quick, as we are once again limited to the amount of rooms available. Price is 2.60 Euros per person per night. Contact Vic on vicfarrer@hotmail.com or telephone his office on (0049) 02161 472 3362.

FOOD
Breakfast is on site from 0730 onwards on the Saturday and Sunday. The bar will stay open until 2300 hours on Friday and Saturday evening. Order your Saturday evening curry when you arrive. This year the Club will once again take charge of messing so its back to those mouth watering delicacies!


Free entrance. Of course donations for the club will be most welcome!
